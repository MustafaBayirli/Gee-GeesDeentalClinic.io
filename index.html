About Us

We are Gee-Gees Dental Clinic - your neighbourhood dentists who are there for you when you need us most!
We proudly help the Ottawa community achieve bright, healthy smiles through a personalized approach to dentistry. With a clean, comfortable environment, welcoming staff, and leading technology in cosmetic dentistry, we ensure that each and every visit is a special and positive experience that gives you the results you want.

Personalized services

We take the time to consult with each and every client in order to fully understand their unique dental needs and tailor our approach.

Services

All of our dental clinics across Ottawa, Gatineau are fully equipped with the latest in dental care technology, offering a wide range of services.



General Dentistry

Our clinics provide children's dentistry, family dentistry, dental cleanings, and dental screenings.

Orthodontics

From diagnosing overbites, occlusions, misaligned teeth and jaws, and overcrowded mouths.

Restorative Dentistry

From dental implants, root canals, dental bridges, dental crowns to wisdom teeth removal.





Periodontics

Periodontics We specialize in periodontics, and in the placement of dental implants.

Sedation Dentistry

Our dentists are able to administer sedatives for particularly anxious patients, or for procedures involving dental surgeries.

Cosmetic Dentistry

From Invisalign, teeth whitening, dental veneers, dentures, to all-on-four.
